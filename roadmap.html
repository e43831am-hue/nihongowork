THE TURNING POINT
Pass N3 and
Japan is within reach.
JLPT N3 is widely recognised as the minimum level for professional employment in Japan. At this level you can understand the main points of texts on everyday topics, follow standard-speed conversation, and communicate clearly in most workplace situations. For Bangladeshi engineers and technical workers, N3 combined with a specialist skill is a powerful combination.
✅
Most Japanese companies list N3 or above as the minimum language requirement for technical and skilled worker visas.
🏗
The Specified Skilled Worker (特定技能) visa — the most accessible route for Bangladeshi workers — requires N4 for the visa but N3 for most actual hiring.
⚡
Electrical workers with the 電気工事士 licence and N3 Japanese are in active demand. Our specialised module prepares you for both simultaneously.
💴
Average monthly salary in Japan for skilled workers: ¥200,000–¥280,000 (approx. ৳180,000–৳250,000). Significantly higher than comparable roles in Bangladesh.

Specified Skilled Worker Visa (特定技能)
This visa allows Bangladeshi nationals to work in Japan in 12 designated industries. Requirements: pass the Japanese Language Test (N4 minimum, N3 preferred) + pass the relevant industry skills test. Duration: up to 5 years, renewable.
💡
The 電気工事士 (Electrical Worker) licence exam is available in Bangladesh through JITCO-certified test centres. Passing it alongside JLPT N3 gives you a two-credential advantage that very few candidates have.
